Welcome to participate in this experiment. The whole experiment consists of four sessions and each will last for around 5min. In each session, you are required to answer EVERY question.

Teaching robots to play sports

Now assuming you have a team of four robots for the robot Olympic game (as shown below). In order to get in the competition, you have to teach these robots how to play different sports. Suppose you recruit professional coaches to help teach and train them remotely. Your task is to rate your team members' performances during the teaching process, and evaluate whether they are good learners.

For each team member (the robot), it needs to study one sports skill in three steps. First of all, a professional coach will demonstrate offline how to do that sports skill ( Check video Step 1 Demonstration ). Second, after the demonstration, the robot will be trained by an coach avatar in an online environment ( Check video Step 2 Learning ). During this process, you need to rate the robot's learning performance. Finally, based on the robot behaviors, you need to estimate whether it can successfully master the skill.

One sample question:

  • The robot was engaged in learning the sports skill.

Good, you can start to teach the robots right now. Please click next to start.
