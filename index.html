Teaching robots to play sports

Now assuming you have a team of four robots for the robot Olympic game. In order to get in the competition, you have to teach these robots how to play different sports. So you recruit professional coaches to help teach and train them remotely. Your task is to rate your team members' performances during the teaching process, and evaluate whether they are good learners.

For each team member (the robot), it needs to study one sports skill in three steps. First of all, a professional coach will demonstrate offline how to do that sport skill. Second, after the demonstration, the robot needs to be coached in an online environment. During this process, you need to rate the robot's learning performances. Finally, the robot needs to perform the skill by itself so that you can evaluate whether it has successfully mastered the skill.
